# Reading Framework

This framework guides the reading of every novel in the Modernist Atlas. It provides a consistent approach to identifying significant formal, conceptual, and critical insights, and to generating permanent notes that contribute to the Atlas's interconnected knowledge base.

## Questions

1. What is the novel's central formal or conceptual problem?

2. What are the novel's principal formal or structural principles?

3. Which recurring images, symbols, motifs, or patterns give the novel coherence?

4. How are individuals and their relationships represented?

5. How does the novel engage with its historical, cultural, political, or intellectual context?

6. What formal innovations distinguish the novel?

7. Which passages are essential for future research?

8. What new concepts or permanent ideas should be extracted into Atlas notes?

9. What connections does this novel have with other texts in the Atlas?
