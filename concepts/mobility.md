# Mobility

## Definition

Mobility refers to the movement of bodies through physical, social, and cultural space. In modernist studies, mobility is not simply movement itself, but movement shaped by structures of power.

---

## Why it matters

Questions of mobility help explain how modernist writers represent:

- freedom and constraint
- class
- gender
- empire
- urban experience
- identity
