# Roadmap

The Modernist Atlas is built gradually, one month at a time. Year One focuses on establishing a broad foundation in modernist studies through theory, primary texts, and open-access scholarship.

## Year One: Foundations

### Month 1 – What is Modernism?

**Theory**

- Christopher Butler, *Modernism: A Very Short Introduction*

**Primary texts**

- James Joyce, *Dubliners*
- Virginia Woolf, *Jacob's Room*

**Reading**

- Two open-access journal articles each week.

**Outputs**

- Permanent notes on the theory book.
- Permanent notes on each primary text.
- Permanent notes on journal articles.
